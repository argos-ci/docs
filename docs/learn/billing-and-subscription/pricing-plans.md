# Pricing plans

Argos offers plans for individuals, teams, and enterprises — from the free Hobby plan to fully custom Enterprise options. For current details, see the [pricing page](https://argos-ci.com/pricing).

### Hobby plan

Best for individuals and small projects.

* **Price**: Free
* **Screenshots included**: 5,000 / month
* **Extra screenshots**: Not available

### Pro plan

Designed for teams, with usage-based pricing.

* **Starting price**: $100 / month
* **Screenshots included**: 35,000 / month
* **Extra screenshots**:
  * Standard: $0.004 per screenshot
  * Storybook: $0.0015 per screenshot

You can subscribe to the Pro plan with a credit card through [Stripe](how-to-subscribe.md), or through [GitHub Marketplace](https://github.com/marketplace/argos-ci) to pay via your GitHub invoice. Marketplace plans come in fixed sizes (S, M, L, XL) with increasing screenshot limits instead of usage-based billing — see [Usage & spend management](spend-management.md) for how each model handles going over the limit.

### Open-source plan

Argos sponsors selected [open-source projects](open-source.md) with free usage, evaluated case by case.

### Enterprise plan

For large organizations with custom requirements: custom screenshot volumes, [SAML single sign-on](../account-and-access/saml-single-sign-on.md), and [fine-grained access control](../account-and-access/team-members-and-roles.md).

[Contact us](https://argos-ci.com/contact) to design the right plan for your business.
